Engage your attendees

Capstory provides the best live experience for your corporate event. Every photo taken can be livestreamed to a projector or TV.

Capstory works without any app required and on any phone, tablet or computer.


Here's how it works:

  • 1. Take a photo or video

    Capstory supports any cell phone or tablet on any carrier in the United States.

    2. Text message OR email

    Text message (just like you would to a friend) or email your photo(s) directly to your Capstory address. Ex. demo@capstory.me

    3. Watch your photo show up!

    Watch the photo show up instantly on the Capstory page livestream. This can be connected to a projector or TV to turn the entire event into an interactive experience!

Package Details

1. Livestream Slideshow

Capstory provides the best live experience for your event. As attendees take photos and simply text message them to Capstory, they show up live on any TV or projector. There's no app for them to download or service to sign up for and it works on any phone or tablet!

2. Remote Moderation

During the entirety of your event, our professionals observe and moderate the photostream. Based on your standards, we ensure nothing inappropriate ever makes it to your photostream and instead log it separately for you to evaluate after the event to give you - and your company - the ultimate piece of mind.

3. Custom Capstory Page

Everyone's photos, videos, and stories are all saved in one shared location online. They are accessible to view and download by your entire team and attendees and you have full access to all the high resolution photos sent in for no additional charge.

4. Priority Support

The Capstory team will work with you every step of the way to ensure your experience is as effortless as possible. We handle setting up your Capstory page, designing and mailing you your instruction cards and even contacting your venue to ensure they have the proper screen and computer setup for Capstory. We are available via phone or email anytime you need us.
